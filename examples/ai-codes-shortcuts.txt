6 AI "codes" that instantly upgrade any prompt. Save this one.

These are the little shortcuts power users type to steer AI fast:

1. ELI5
"Explain like I'm 5." Add it when the answer is too jargon-heavy and you want it dead simple.

2. TLDR
Caps a rambling answer at one or two sentences. Use it when you want the point, not the essay.

3. Truth mode / kill the critic
Tells the AI to stop being agreeable and give you honest critique. The fix for AI that just flatters you.

4. Step by step
Forces it to break the process into real steps instead of a vague paragraph. Better answers every time.

5. Steel man it
Makes the AI argue the strongest version of the other side. The fastest way to pressure-test your own opinion.

6. Ask me 5 questions first, then push back
Add this and the AI clarifies instead of guessing. Saves you three rounds of back-and-forth.

Six small phrases. Big difference in the output. Use one in your next prompt.
