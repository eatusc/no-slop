Case study: AmzDash - the Amazon ads dashboard we built for our clients.

The problem: Amazon ads data is noisy. One bad week makes sellers pause winners and scale losers.

What we built:

- Pulls every campaign + every search term daily via Amazon's Ads API and SP-API, not the console
- Dashboard with week-over-week deltas on every metric
- 3 AI analysts review the account in parallel: last 7 days, last 14, full month
- A 4th AI cross-references them - only findings confirmed by 2+ timeframes survive
- Output: negative keywords, wasted spend, problem campaigns, winners to scale, ranked with expected impact

One bad week isn't a trend. Three timeframes keep each other honest.
