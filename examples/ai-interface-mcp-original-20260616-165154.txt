No more apps or websites?

Maybe not literally.

But the interface is changing fast.

People are already going straight to chatbots instead of digging through websites.

And the plumbing is here now.

MCP lets AI connect to an app's real data and actions.
Anthropic shipped it in 2024.
OpenAI and Google followed.

That flips the product question.

The app becomes the backend.
The AI becomes the interface.

For builders, the question is no longer just:

"What's our app?"

It's:

"What can the AI call?"

Be the capability, not the destination.
