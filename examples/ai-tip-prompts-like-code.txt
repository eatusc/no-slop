AI tip: treat your prompts like code.

People who are good at AI are not starting from scratch every time.

They save what worked.
They turn it into a reusable template.
They version it after strong runs.
They turn repeated workflows into skills.

The move:

1. Make one home for your best prompts.
2. Structure each one like a function: goal, role, constraints, steps, output format, examples.
3. Bump the version when you improve it.
4. Turn repeat workflows into reusable skills.

Everyone else reinvents the wheel in every new chat.

You build on your best version.

That gap compounds.
