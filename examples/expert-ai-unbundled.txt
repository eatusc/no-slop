Unpopular opinion: for most standard questions, AI is already good enough to replace the first pass from an expert.

Not the weird edge case.
Not the high-stakes judgment call.
Not the final signature.

But the standard answer?
The explanation?
The second opinion?
The "what am I missing?"

That got unbundled.

The real rule is simple:

Use AI for the standard answer.
Use humans for the weird, high-stakes call.

That is not anti-expert.
It is just a better use of experts.
