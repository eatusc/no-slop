ThrivePact is not just a habit tracker.

It is a behavior loop:

- make a pact
- invite the crew
- check in every day
- see who showed up
- keep the streak alive
- roll into the next 7-day segment

The interesting part was not the landing page.

It was the product logic underneath: OTP auth, Supabase/Postgres with RLS, invite flows, host approval, photo check-ins, streaks, late logs, time zones, notification dedupe, SMS, payments, and weekly settlement mechanics.

That is the difference between building screens and building a product that can hold pressure.

For ThrivePact, trust is the feature.

If reminders fire twice, the product feels spammy.
If a timezone is wrong, the streak feels unfair.
If the check-in flow breaks, the whole behavior loop breaks.

That is where the build gets real.
