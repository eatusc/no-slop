I've basically stopped watching tutorials.

Not because tutorials are useless.

Because for pure information, most how-to videos are a slow interface.

A 12-minute answer to a 30-second question.

Now I ask AI directly:

"Explain this simply."
"Show me."
"Walk me through the steps."
"Give me a diagram."
"Find me the exact link."
"If there's a good video, give me that too."

The difference is interruption.

You can ask why.
You can slow down.
You can skip what you already know.
You can make it explain the same idea three different ways.

A video can't do that.
