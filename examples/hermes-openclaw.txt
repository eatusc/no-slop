Hermes and OpenClaw look incredible. Most people still don't need them.

They are also not what most people need.

The pitch sounds incredible:

- always-on agent
- texts you first
- remembers everything
- manages your inbox
- runs local models

But most actual use cases are still email cleanup, reminders, lead lists, summaries, and basic workflow help.

ChatGPT and Claude already do most of that without turning your weekend into a command-line debugging session.

If you really need an always-on local agent today, eat the setup pain.

If you do not, wait.

The big models will absorb a lot of this natively.

Sometimes the best AI purchase is no purchase.
