Case study: a marketing S-corp was paying every month for bookkeeping and reconciliation software.

We replaced the stack with one dashboard they own.

It handles monthly P&L, bank reconciliation, payroll, federal and CA filings, year-end W-2s and K-1s, tax deposit tracking, and deadline visibility.

The CPA stayed in the loop. The app captures, calculates, and organizes. The accountant verifies and signs.

That is the pattern I like: remove recurring software cost without removing human judgment.

A forever subscription became a one-time build.
