How to build a fast AI phone agent in GoHighLevel. The simple version. Save it.

Steps:

1. Sign up for GoHighLevel and start the free trial:
https://www.gohighlevel.com

2. Settings > Phone Numbers > buy a number.
This is the line your AI answers.

3. Build the Voice AI agent.
Give it a name, a one-line greeting, your business info, and one clear job: book the appointment or qualify the lead.

4. Make the voice fast.
Native GHL Voice AI is the simplest path because it handles the voice stack for you.

For more control, run the agent in Vapi and connect it to GHL calendars and workflows:
https://docs.vapi.ai/tools/go-high-level
https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000007349-vapi-actions-and-triggers-in-workflows

Fast stack:
Hear: Deepgram Nova-3
Think: GPT-4o-mini, or your current preferred low-latency mini model
Speak: Cartesia Sonic or ElevenLabs Flash v2.5
Target: under 700ms round trip when your full network, STT, LLM, TTS, and streaming path is tuned well.

5. Route the number to the agent and lower the inbound call timeout.
HighLevel says if the caller reaches local voicemail, the Voice AI agent will not answer the call.
https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000003431-inbound-call-flow-for-voice-ai-calls
https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000006881-phone-number-edit-configuration-incoming-calls-settings-

6. Give it hands in Workflows.
Update the contact, book on your calendar, transfer to a human when needed, and text a recap after the call.

7. Test it.
Use GHL's built-in Voice AI tester, call your own agent, try to stump it, then tune.
https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004108-testing-voice-ai-agents
